Pathaan Movie

Starring: Shah Rukh Khan

"Pathaan" is a 2023 Hindi action thriller where an exiled RAW agent, Pathaan, teams up with an ISI agent to stop a rogue former agent from unleashing a deadly virus on India. Starring Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, and John Abraham, it's part of the YRF Spy Universe.

Ra.One Movie

Starring: Shah Rukh Khan

"Ra.One" is a 2011 Indian superhero film where a video game villain, Ra.One, escapes into the real world to target Prateek, the son of the game's creator, Shekhar Subramaniam. After Ra.One kills Shekhar, Prateek brings the game's hero, G.One, who resembles his father, to life to defeat Ra.One and protect his family. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan in dual roles, with Arjun Rampal as the antagonist.

Tiger 3 Movie

Starring: Salman Khan

"Tiger 3" is a 2023 Hindi action thriller directed by Maneesh Sharma, starring Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, and Emraan Hashmi. As the fifth installment in the YRF Spy Universe and a sequel to "Ek Tha Tiger" and "Tiger Zinda Hai," the film follows Tiger and Zoya as they battle Zoya's former mentor, rogue ISI agent Aatish Rehman. Aatish manipulates them to stage a coup against the Pakistani government while pursuing a destructive agenda against India. The story also explores the origins of Tiger and Zoya's connection.

Ghajini Movie

Starring: Aamir Khan

"Ghajini" is a 2008 Hindi action thriller directed by A. R. Murugadoss, starring Aamir Khan, Asin Thottumkal, Jiah Khan, and Pradeep Rawat. A remake of Murugadoss's Tamil film, it follows Sanjay Singhania, a powerful entrepreneur who suffers from anterograde amnesia after a brutal attack on him and his fiancée, Kalpana. The film marks Asin's debut in Hindi cinema and is one of Jiah Khan's final films before her death in 2013.

When Ajay slaps a student, Atul Raghuvanshi, out of frustration, he discovers Atul is the son of a powerful MLA. Fearing retribution, Ajay impulsively plans a trip to significant World War II sites in Europe, taking Nisha along in hopes of resolving their marital issues. This journey transforms their perspectives, offering them profound lessons on life, history, and their relationship.

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jaayenge Movie

Starring: Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol

"Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" (DDLJ) is a 1995 Hindi musical romance directed by Aditya Chopra in his debut and produced by Yash Chopra. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol as Raj and Simran, two young non-resident Indians who fall in love during a European vacation. When Simran's father insists she marry the man he promised her to, Raj sets out to win her family's approval so they can be together. Filmed in India, London, and Switzerland, DDLJ has become a classic in Indian cinema.
